tips & tricks

iPhones that have FaceID offer a feature that can be buried or forgotten: Turn Attention Aware features on or off on your iPhone or iPad Pro. This setting says: `iPhone will check for attention before dimming the display, expanding a notification when locked, or lowering the volume of some alerts.' If your alarms are surprisingly at low volume, you might want to turn off this option.

A reminder: Apple offers the option for additional Stolen Device Protection (in Settings > FaceID Stolen Device Protection). This can sometimes mean you get forestalled when trying to add a face to FaceID while traveling. This happened to Olga recently while flying. She tried to train FaceID to recognize her with an additional set of glasses. But she had to wait for 60 minutes to do so.

gadgets & accessories

The QWERKYWRITER is a wireless keyboard that is meant to remind you of old typewriters. It has an integrated stand for your iPhone or iPad.

A favorite keyboard for many developers is the Flow84, bt Lofree. It is a low-profile mechanical keyboard, extremely slim and thin design, made to be very durable due to a aluminum-alloy design for long-term use. It is white backlight and with an RGB sidelight.

The Logitech Pebble Keys 2 K380s is a wireless keyboard that works with your iPhone or iPad. The NYTimes Wirecutter considers it the best choice for a wireless bluetooth keyboard, writing that `it is comfortable to type on, compact, and delivers long battery life. Plus, it can pair with and switch between three Bluetooth devices.'

the Logitech K585 is another wireless keyboard by Logitech. It includes a number pad and has a cradle for your device. The NYTimes Wirecutter considers it the 2nd best choice for a wireless bluetooth keyboard, writing that `this model has a number pad and a stand for your phone or tablet. Like our top pick, it feels comfortable to type on and offers long battery life.`
