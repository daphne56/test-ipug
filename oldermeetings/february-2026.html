Olga recommends naming your timers. For example, you can say `Hey Siri, set a rice timer for 12 minutes' instead of saying `Hey Siri, set a time for 12 minutes.' Naming your timers gives you better control. For example, you can later say `Hey siri change to rice timer to 15 minutes' and this will happen easily. Additionally, it's a good idea to name your timers because if you have several going at the same time, you will avoid confusion.
