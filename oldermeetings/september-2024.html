new AirPods

Apple unveiled new AirPods 4, with 2 versions and price-points.

Apple announced new AirPods Pro 2, with hearing aide features: Apple introduces groundbreaking health features to support conditions impacting billions of people, Apple announced. These are awaiting FDA approval soon, and the feature will be released via s/w update. Additionally, your watch will be able to detect possible sleep apnea.
