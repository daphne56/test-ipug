tips & tricks

If you are trying to login to a Google account on an iPhone or iPad, you might get an error message that you cannot log in, despite the fact that you are using the correct credentials. Often, the fix is to temporarily disable iCloud Private Relay, login to your Google account, and re-enable Private Relay.
