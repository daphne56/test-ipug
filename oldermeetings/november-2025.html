tips & tricks

If you are considering having a lot of smart devices around the house, there are many choices in the market. It's a really good idea to stick with those which support Apple's HomeKit. Additionally, it's worth considering those that have a bridge. The bridge connects to your home WiFi, and the accessories connect to the bridge. Olga reported getting a new WiFi after the old one died. It was much, much easier to connect the accessories that used a bridge than the individual accessories.
