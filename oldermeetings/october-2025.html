tips & tricks

Recall that on the iPhone, if you use the calculator in landscape mode you can enable scientific mode. But with iOS 26, you can select the mode you want even in portrait mode.

The Hack I’m Using to Clear Out My Camera Roll at Last, writes the NYTimes. The hack is very simple and clever every day, do a search in your Photo app for all photos that match today's date. Review the results and delete the ones you don't need. This spreads the reviews to a limited set so it's not overwhelming. Over time, you will have reviewed all your photos. (In fact, you might feel inspired to do a few extra days at a time, speeding things up, but this method does not ask for too much at any time.)
