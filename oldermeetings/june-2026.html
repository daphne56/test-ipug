iOS apps

Do you sometimes need a secondary cell number, such as when you sign up for something, but don't want to be inundated with messages on your regular number? Consider TextNow. It's an app that runs on your iPhone and gives you a mobile number. You can preserve the number for $3.99 per year, or $1.99 per week. They offer other in-app purchases, but you don't need anything more.

tips & tricks

If you need a second phone number, 3rd party apps like TextNow mentioned above, can do the trick. But if you need a temporary or disposable email address, remember that Apple offers this to all iCloud+ users. Olga recommends it highly. She currently has 133 email addresses (they are aliases for her single iCloud address). Go to Settings > (your name) > iCloud and scroll to iCloud+ features > Hide My Email.

general apps & services

The web service Privnote.com lets you write and send a note to someone or to a group that is private. As soon as it is read, it is automatically deleted. Or you can have it deleted within a period of time, such as 24 hours. This works with regular text, not images or other files. The service is free.

The website everynoise.com gives you a visual and interactive map of all music genres across the world.
