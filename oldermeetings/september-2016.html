new Apple Watch

The new Apple Watch Series 2 has faster CPU, has GPS, is water resistant to 50 meters ( = 150 feet).

The speaker on the watch `pushes' out water when underwater!

The original watch is getting the same new CPU upgrade and becomes Series 1, at a lower price point.

iMore takes hands-on look at the new watch, says the ceramic model is stunning.

The dimensions are effectively identical as the original but very slightly thicker and slightly heavier, due to the larger battery.

AirPods

Apple introduced a new product, the AirPods. These are wireless versions of their classic earpods but with a lot of impressive intelligence.

Hands-on with AirPods: they stayed in my ears and sounded awesome, writes Susie Ochs for Macworld.
